About Me

I'm an English Education Student

My name is Nabila Zefa Herta Utami, a student of English Education at Universitas Bengkulu. I have a basic understanding of Arabic from my time at a pesantren in middle school, which enriched my communication skills and cultural awareness. With a strong ability to memorize, I excel at learning new materials, and one of my achievements includes winning a Kitab Kuning reading competition. As the firstborn of two siblings and someone born prematurely, I am determined to pursue a career in international education to advance inclusive and impactful language teaching.
In my free time, I enjoy traveling, reading novels, listening to NCT Dream’s music, and exploring photography. I also love playing badminton to stay active and indulging in sweet treats like chocolate, although I’m not a fan of vegetables. These hobbies inspire creativity and balance in my life while supporting my passion for growth and learning.
